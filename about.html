About Us

Weigh & Whisk was founded by Lea-Anne Gaffney. She is a pastry chef and already has an established blog called Weigh & Whisk where she shares her creations and recipes. Living in Ireland she found that there was a gap in the market for her business idea. She wanted people located anywhere in the Republic of Ireland to be able to plan a party from the one website and have their order delivered to their door. She wanted it to be a "one stop shop for all your party needs".

Instead of having to visit many different websites to order the cake, balloons and party essentials customers can now do it all from the one website. With the help of her team of bakers Lea-Anne is proud of every sweet treat that leaves the bakery and hopes that her customers enjoy eating them as much as she enjoys creating them.

If you have any questions about our service or even have any ideas of what you'd like to see available on Weigh & Whisk please do not hesitate to contact us here.
